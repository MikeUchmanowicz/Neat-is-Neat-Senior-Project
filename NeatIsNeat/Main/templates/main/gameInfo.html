Swim Fish, Swim

Game Info

(As a Player)

  • The game is written on a local python script.
  • The game will be a 2D "scroller" game.
  • You will play as a fish in the ocean.
  • You will have one control input: spacebar to swim upwards. No input will cause you to descend.
  • You will be tasked with avoiding obstacles (fishermen & sharks) as long as possble.
  • Colliding with an obstacle will end the game.
  • The further your distance within the game, the higher your score.
  • Collecting worms will increase your score.
